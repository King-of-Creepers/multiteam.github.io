Basic technology

Let's get started!

Here we are in our first world with factory automaton, but how to start? How we get any kind of resources? Now, we are here to tell you!

Basic technology: Starter age Iron age

Rocks in the world

You started punching a tree huh? I was right... Before you can chop down trees, you need some tool to do it. First, pick up some rocks from the ground with a simple click.


The chopping blade

After you searched for some rocks and flint, you can craft your first tool, the chopping blade. With that tool the tree-punching will not hurt you :D

The flint also generated on the surface like rocks


The chopping block

To convert logs into planks you need a workstation to do it, this is the chopping block. To get it, right click on a wood log's top. The log can be any type.


Recipes of the chopping block

To use the chopping block, right click on with an item, and start left-clicking on it.
Recipes:
-Wood log Wooden planks
-Wooden planks Sticks
-Grass Plant fiber


Time to mine

The next thing you should want, is start mining. But to start, you will need a pickaxe. Here comes the Flint pickaxe. This item is a replacment of the vanilla wooden pickaxe. Now you can mine some stone, to make a better pickaxe.


New ores

In the mines, you will find some new ores. The Cassiterite wich is tin, and Malachite wich is copper.


Smelting ores

To smelt ores, you cant use your regular old furnace. You need the basic foundry, wich is a multiblock. To build it, you will need a furnace, a stone crucible and a casting vessel. Place the crucible on the top of the furnace. Right click on the crucible to activate it. And then palce the vessel beside the furnace, under the casting tunnel.


Usage of the foundry I.

To use the foundry, you need a lot of coal. To make patterns to cast things like ingots, nuggets or stuff like that, you need green sand. To craft geen sand you need 2 clay block and 2 sand block. Right click to put the green sand into the vessel, and then right click on the filled vessel using a stick to open the user interface.


Usage of the foundry II.

Now, you can start melting down your ores, but not all of them. The basic foundry will only accept copper and tin. And just a tip: if the foundry is full, dont put more ore in it, because its gonna go to waste.


Usage of the foundry III.

To cast something, after you select the pattern, right click on the crucible at the casting channel. Just a tip: Dont try to pick the casted item too fast, because its very hot. It need to cool down.

After this, you have your basic metal ingots.


Tier 2 craftingtable

For advanced tools, like hammers, you will need a more advanced workstation than the old crafting table. Here comes in the Tier 2 workbench. To craft it, you will need 4 copper or tin ingot, stone slabs and a crafting table.
