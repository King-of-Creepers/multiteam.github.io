Garden Arsenal v0.3.0 by MultiTeam

A mod that adds fun things, like weapons that shoots various vegetables and fruits, and sometimes food items, by MulitTeam in collaboration with VampireStudios
v0.3.0 is the rewrite for MC 1.16.4/5 Forge & Fabric. Alongside the rewritten code, all assets and textures has been reworked.
