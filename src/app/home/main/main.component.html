All about




Our group was founded by @[László Gaming]#3764 aka LazTheArtist back in 2017.

We started as a minecraft modding group, but we grew larger by making games since 2020. There were tries before that, but officially its 2020.

Back in the day, i aka Laz, wanted to make minecraft mods, but i wasnt able to code myself, so i began searching for someone who can. Basically this is how MultiTeam was founded; It consists of me, and those who could code minecraft mods. We had started several mods, and some are still being worked on today. But in 2020 came the change, specially in the last quarter. I started to work on a game with my friends in the first quarter of 2020, learned a bit of C# and unty itself, and the idea of Multiteam being more than a minecraft modding group started forming. Later in the year, near the third or fourth quarter, i started working on another game, and as we gone along, i finally wanted to make it official: We are no longer just a minecraft modding group. This is how this site was renewed in december of 2020, and basically the whole idea of Multiteam.
