About the mod

This is a mod about technology, with nice looking factory machines. This mod brings age system to the game, this includs pre-stone age, stone age, metal age [copper and tin], bronze age, iron age, steel age, electryc age.

Supported languages:
-English (US)
-Hungary (HU)
If you want your language in the mod, come and translate! And we will add to the mod!
