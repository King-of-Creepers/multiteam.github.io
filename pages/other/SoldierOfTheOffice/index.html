Soldier of the Office is a 2D platformer game, about a soldier who fights with various crazy weapons and enemys.

To open the tabs, use the buttons in the header

This game is being developed on Unity (2020.1), by Multiteam: LazTheArtist [Code/Art], and many ideas given by @ZoLeY#4832 and @DragonsMoon3#2285.
Devlogs

2020 january
[#0000] Initiation - Player movement ⯈
Intial game set up, basic collisions, basic player movement. air control will be enabled.
⯇ (click to open)
[#0001] Basic Gun mechanics (nailgun) ⯈
Nailguns added, and can be picked up by the player. tho it originally used a dumb way to do that, since it was rewritten. Pressing Q will pick up weapons.
⯇

2020 june
[#0002] Picking up objects ⯈
Picking up object has been implemented, with bugs tho. these bugs were eliminated since then. Pressing E while staying next to an object that can be picked up will lift it above the player's head.
⯇
[#0003] Ammo game-mechanic ⯈
Now weapons use ammo. added ammo boxes that store ammo on the ground and if you have a weapon equipped and walk into the box, the ammo will be added to current weapon and if all the ammo gets picked from the box, the box will disappear.
⯇
[#0004] Tooltip system ⯈
To know how many ammo is stored in a weapon without picking it up, just hover over it with your cursor and a tooltip will show up. same thing with ammo boxes.
⯇

2020 july
[#0005] Button and Lamp game-mechanic ⯈
Button game mechanic got added, along with a lamp that detects a button if its pressed or not and turns on/of according to the button's state. the button can be pressed by the player standing on it, and objects that can be picked up by the player.
⯇
[Technical] Engine version update (unity 2020.1.0a17 -> 2020.1)
[#0006] Damage system ⯈
damage system, outgoing damage from the player. added test dummy.
⯇
[#0007] Level 1 Enemy ⯈
damage system vol2, incoming damage to player, health system, death screen
⯇
[#0008] Improved weapon mechanics ⯈
increased bulet drop of the nail shot by the nailgun. added 360 rotation ability to the guns (around the player)
⯇
